About Local JunkMan

A San Diego-based junk removal service built on showing up, doing the job right, and treating every customer's property with respect.

Our Story

A San Diego-Based Business Built Around Real Service

Large cleanout projects can pile up fast — a packed garage, leftover rental contents, estate items, or debris after a renovation. Local JunkMan helps homeowners, property managers, realtors, contractors, and businesses clear the space without handling the heavy lifting themselves.

Local JunkMan is not a franchise or chain. It is an owner-operated, San Diego-based junk removal company focused on professional service, clear estimates, and larger cleanout projects across San Diego County.

Instead of tossing everything into a landfill, Local JunkMan makes sustainability a priority. Items that can be reused are donated, and recyclable materials are carefully sorted — an eco-friendly approach that has made the company a trusted choice for residents who want junk removal that's both effective and responsible.

We are licensed and insured, and we take pride in every cleanout project — from garages and rentals to estates, commercial spaces, and construction debris.

The Story Behind the Work

Meet Shane Connor

Shane Connor is a San Diego native who built Local JunkMan from the ground up. After years working in construction and demolition, he saw a real need for a local junk removal company that shows up on time, communicates clearly, and treats every customer's property with respect.

He started Local JunkMan to serve his community the right way — with a licensed, insured, professional operation that San Diego County could count on. Every job reflects that same standard.

That commitment is why Local JunkMan has earned a perfect 5.0 rating across every Google review — and why customers keep coming back.
