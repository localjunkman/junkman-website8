Questions

Before you book a pickup.

Text us if you don't see your question here. We'll give you a straight answer.

How do I get a junk removal quote?

Call or text 619-990-5684 with a short description or photo of what needs to go. We’ll review the items, access, and pickup details, then send a clear custom quote.

Do I need to move items outside first?

No. We can remove items from inside your home, garage, yard, storage unit, office, or job site. Point, and we do the lifting.

Do you offer same-day service?

Same-day and next-day pickup are available when the schedule allows. Text or call early for the best chance.

What items do you not take?

Some regulated or unsafe materials may require a specialized provider. If you're unsure, text us a photo before scheduling.

Do you donate or recycle?

Whenever practical, usable items are donated and recyclable materials are routed to proper facilities.

What areas do you serve?

We serve San Diego County, including La Jolla, UTC, Mission Valley, Point Loma, Rancho Santa Fe, San Marcos, Carlsbad, Encinitas, Escondido, Chula Vista, La Mesa, and nearby areas.
